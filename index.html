Kisha's Story - Life with Atopic Dermatitis

Role: Project Lead (2-person team)
Description:
This project brought Kisha's story to life through engaging animation and creative storytelling. Working closely with the client, I led the end-to-end process, which included:

  • Designing characters and stylistic elements to match the client’s vision.
  • Creating vibrant and immersive backgrounds.
  • Developing a detailed storyboard to map out Kisha’s journey visually.
  • Animating the scenes while ensuring smooth transitions and lively movements.
  • Adding sound effects (SFX), music, and voiceovers to enhance the emotional depth.
  • Delivering a final rendered video, perfectly aligned with the client's expectations.

Stage Process:
1. Client briefing and style direction.
2. Character design, background creation, and storyboard development.
3. Scene animation and dynamic transitions.
4. Final rendering with music, sound effects, and voiceovers.
